My Projects

Here are some of the projects I've worked on. These projects showcase my skills in front-end development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. While I am still learning and improving, I am excited to share these projects with you!

Money Management App :

Description: A user-friendly app designed to help individuals track their income, expenses, and manage their finances. The app allows users to set budgets, categorize their expenses, and visualize spending patterns through graphs and charts. It helps users stay on top of their financial goals and make informed decisions about their spending.

Key Features:

  • track income and expenses in real-time
  • Categorize expenses and set budgets
  • Save financial goals and monitor progress

Grocery Shopping Online App:

Description: A comprehensive online grocery shopping app that allows users to browse, shop, and order groceries from local stores. The app features a clean and simple interface, where users can search for products, add them to their cart, and place an order for home delivery or pickup.

Key Features:

  • Browse a wide range of grocery items
  • Add products to a shopping list or cart
  • Secure payment and delivery options

Fitness Tracker App:

Description: A comprehensive app designed to help users track their fitness and wellness journey. It allows users to set fitness goals, monitor their progress. The app aims to encourage users to maintain a healthy lifestyle by providing personalized data and fitness recommendations.

Key Features:

  • Set and track personal fitness goals
  • Log workouts and monitor performance over time
  • Receive personalized fitness recommendations based on activity data
